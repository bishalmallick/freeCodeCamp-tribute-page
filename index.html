Prime Minister Narendra Modi

Prime Minister of 1 Billion people.

Prime Minister Narendra Modi in Red Fort during Republic Day.

Here's a timeline of the life of our Prime Minister:

  • Sept 17, 1950 - Born in Vadnagar in the state of Gujrat.
  • Dec 25, 1965 - India-Pakistan War Breaks Out. Narendra Modi serves tea at Mehsana Railway Station to Jawans.
  • June 03, 1967 - Leaves home and travels across India including the Himalayas, Rishikesh, Ramakrishna Mission.
  • October 03, 1972 - Joins the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh. Takes a conscious decision to devote his entire life towards the mission & vision of RSS.
  • June 03, 1974 - Active participant of Navnirman Movement, a youth movement against corruption in Gujarat.
  • June 03, 1975 - Emergency is imposed. Narendra Modi gets fully involved in anti-Emergency struggle.
  • June 03, 1980 - The Bharatiya Janata Party is born.
  • February 03, 1987 - Modi entered mainstream politics joining BJP in 1987, and was promoted as the General Secretary of the Gujarat unit within a year.
  • March 23, 1995 - BJP came to power individually in Gujarat in 1995 and has its stronghold there ever since. Modi was appointed as the National Secretary of the party then. He was elevated to the post of General Secretary in 1998, and held the position till 2001.
  • October 07, 2001 - Probably the biggest shift to power for the BJP leader was when he was chosen to replace Gujarat's then Chief Minister Keshubhai Patel in 2001 since the latter's health was deteriorating. He was appointed by the then Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee.
  • February 24, 2002 - Modi swore in as the chief minister on October 7, 2001. He was elected to power in a by-poll.
  • December 01, 2002 - Leads BJP to record win in 2002 Gujarat Assembly Elections.
  • June 01, 2007 - Becomes longest serving CM of Gujarat.
  • December 23, 2007 - BJP wins 2007 Gujarat Assembly Elections. Narendra Modi sworn-in as CM for 3rd time.
  • October 07, 2008 - Tata Nano drives into Gujarat. Tata Group lauds Gujarat’s governance mode
  • December 26, 2012 - Leads BJP to yet another victory in the Gujarat Assembly Polls. Becomes CM for 4th time.
  • June 09, 2013 - Named BJP’s Campaign Committee Chief for 2014 Lok Sabha Elections.
  • September 13, 2013 - BJP declares Narendra Modi as the PM candidate of the Party for 2014 Lok Sabha Elections.
  • March 26, 2014 - Begins campaign for 2014 Lok Sabha Elections by addressing Bharat Vijay Rally in Udhampur.
  • May 16, 2014 - Historic victory for NDA in 2014 Elections. Narendra Modi Tweets, "India has won! भारत की विजय।"
  • May 26, 2014 - Sworn in as Prime Minister of India.
  • November 08, 2016 - Historic announcements on ending corruption and black money by introducing Demonetization by PM Narendra Modi.
  • July 01, 2017 - India ushers in GST amid historic midnight session of Parliament.
  • December 30, 2018 - PM attends function to mark 75th anniversary of hoisting of Tricolour on Indian soil by Netaji
  • May 25, 2019 - Shri Narendra Modi elected as the leader of NDA in the Central Hall of Parliament House
  • May 30, 2019 - Shri Narendra Modi takes oath as the Prime Minister for the second term.
  • 5 August 2019 - The historic decison taken by the Government of India under PM Narendra Modi revoked the special status, or limited autonomy, granted under Article 370, which completed the total Integration of India
  • February 05, 2020 - PM Modi announces setting up of 'Shri Ram Janma Bhoomi Tirtha Kshetra' trust
  • March 24, 2020 - PM Modi calls for complete lockdown of entire nation for 21 days
  • August 05, 2020 - PM Modi lays the foundation stone of Shri Ram Jamnabhoomi temple at Ayodhya and performed Bhoomi Pujan.
  • December 29, 2020 - The most important and largest project in Independent India, PM Modi inaugurates New Bhaupur-New Khurja section of Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridor. The game changer project for India.
